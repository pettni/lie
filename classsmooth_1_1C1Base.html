Detailed Description

template<typename _Derived>
class smooth::C1Base< _Derived >

Base class for C1 Lie group types.

Memory layout

  • Group: \( \mathbf{x} = [a, b] \)
  • Tangent: \( \mathbf{a} = [s, \omega_z] \)

Constraints

  • Group: \( a^2 + b^2 > 0 \)
  • Tangent: \( -\pi < \omega_z \leq \pi \)

Lie group matrix form

\[ \mathbf{X} = \begin{bmatrix} b & -a \\ a & b \end{bmatrix} \in \mathbb{R}^{2 \times 2} \]

Lie algebra matrix form

\[ \mathbf{a}^\wedge = \begin{bmatrix} s & -\omega_z \\ \omega_z & s \\ \end{bmatrix} \in \mathbb{R}^{2 \times 2} \]
