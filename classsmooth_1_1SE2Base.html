Detailed Description

template<typename _Derived>
class smooth::SE2Base< _Derived >

Base class for SE2 Lie group types.

Internally represented as \(\mathbb{U}(1) \times \mathbb{R}^2\).

Memory layout

  • Group: \( \mathbf{x} = [x, y, q_z, q_w] \)
  • Tangent: \( \mathbf{a} = [v_x, v_y, \omega_z] \)

Constraints

  • Group: \(q_z^2 + q_w^2 = 1 \)
  • Tangent: \( -\pi < \omega_z \leq \pi \)

Lie group matrix form

\[ \mathbf{X} = \begin{bmatrix} q_w & -q_z & x \\ q_z & q_w & y \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \in \mathbb{R}^{3 \times 3} \]

Lie algebra matrix form

\[ \mathbf{a}^\wedge = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & -\omega_z & v_x \\ \omega_z & 0 & v_y \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix} \in \mathbb{R}^{3 \times 3} \]
